<p><p>Ion nitriding services utilize different gas mixtures to enhance the hardness and wear resistance of metal components. The "Nitrogen Hydrogen Mixture" involves combining nitrogen with hydrogen, which assists in creating a reactive environment that improves diffusion and results in a smoother surface finish. In contrast, the "Nitrogen Argon Mixture" uses argon to provide inert shielding during the nitriding process, minimizing oxidation while maintaining effective nitrogen penetration. Both mixtures optimize the ion nitriding process for various industrial applications.</p></p>
