<p><strong>The Automotive MEMS Inertial Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Accelerometer</li><li>MEMS Gyroscope</li><li>MEMS IMU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive MEMS inertial sensor market comprises three key types: MEMS accelerometers, MEMS gyroscopes, and MEMS inertial measurement units (IMUs). MEMS accelerometers detect linear acceleration, enabling functionalities like collision detection and stability control. MEMS gyroscopes measure angular velocity, which aids in navigation and vehicle stability. MEMS IMUs combine both accelerometers and gyroscopes, providing comprehensive motion sensing for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicles. These sensors enhance vehicle safety, performance, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
