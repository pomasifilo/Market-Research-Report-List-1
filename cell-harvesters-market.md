<p><p>The cell harvesters market can be categorized into two main types: displacement collectors and therapy collectors. Displacement collectors are designed to efficiently gather cells by creating a flow that physically moves them from one area to another, often utilized in laboratory settings for research purposes. Therapy collectors, on the other hand, focus on the collection of cells intended for therapeutic applications, such as regenerative medicine or cell-based treatments, emphasizing sterility and viability for optimal patient outcomes.</p></p>
