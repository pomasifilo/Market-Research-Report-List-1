<p><p>The failsafe spring brake market is categorized into two primary types: double spring brakes and single spring brakes. Double spring brakes utilize two springs, providing enhanced reliability and braking force, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, single spring brakes rely on one spring, offering a lighter and more cost-effective solution while still ensuring safety. Both types are essential in automotive and industrial sectors for emergency applications, ensuring that vehicles or machinery can stop effectively in case of a failure.</p></p>
