<p><p>The Collateralized Debt Obligation (CDO) market includes several types: </p><p>1. Collateralized Loan Obligations (CLOs) focus on pooling loans, especially corporate loans, and selling tranches to investors.</p><p>2. Collateralized Bond Obligations (CBOs) involve bonds, where cash flows from underlying bonds fund the tranches.</p><p>3. Collateralized Synthetic Obligations (CSOs) utilize derivatives for exposure to a portfolio of loans or bonds without actual ownership.</p><p>4. Structured Finance CDOs (SFCDOs) bundle various structured finance products, offering diversified investments in complex structures, enhancing risk and return profiles.</p></p>
