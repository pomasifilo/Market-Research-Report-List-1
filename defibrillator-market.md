<p><strong>The Defibrillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic External Defibrillators</li><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators</li><li>Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillator</li><li>Advanced Life Support (ALS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillator market comprises several key types. Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) are portable devices used in emergencies to treat cardiac arrest by delivering shocks. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically placed in patients at risk of life-threatening arrhythmias, providing constant monitoring. Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs) are external devices worn by patients to detect arrhythmias and deliver shocks if necessary. Advanced Life Support (ALS) defibrillators are used by healthcare professionals and include features for critical care situations, supporting complex rescue operations.</p></p>
