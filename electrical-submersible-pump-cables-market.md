<p><p>The Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) cables market features two primary types: EPDM (Synthetic Rubber) cables and Polypropylene (Plastic) cables. EPDM cables are known for their excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and moisture, making them suitable for harsh submersible environments. In contrast, Polypropylene cables offer lightweight and flexible options, with good chemical resistance and lower production costs. Both types cater to the demands of various industries, ensuring reliable performance and durability in diverse pumping applications.</p></p>
