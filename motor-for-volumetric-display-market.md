<p><p>The volumetric display market features two key types of technologies: Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS). DLP technology utilizes micro mirrors to project images with high brightness and contrast, ideal for dynamic content. In contrast, LCOS technology employs liquid crystal panels on silicon substrates, providing superior resolution and color accuracy, making it suitable for detailed visual applications. Both technologies cater to diverse sectors, enhancing presentations, simulations, and entertainment experiences through advanced volumetric imaging.</p></p>
