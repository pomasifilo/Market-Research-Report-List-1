<p><p>The respiratory humidification devices market comprises three main types: invasive ventilation, non-invasive ventilation, and high-flow nasal cannula (HFNC). Invasive ventilation involves mechanical support through an artificial airway, often used in critical care. Non-invasive ventilation delivers respiratory assistance via masks, aiding patients without intubation. HFNC provides high-flow oxygen through nasal cannulas, offering effective humidification and comfort for patients with respiratory distress. Together, these devices enhance patient care by reducing airway dryness and improving overall respiratory function.</p></p>
