<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight (ToF) Camera Module market is generally categorized into two types: direct and indirect. Direct ToF involves capturing the time it takes for light pulses emitted by the camera to return after hitting an object, enabling real-time depth mapping. Indirect ToF, on the other hand, uses modulated light and analyzes phase shifts, often resulting in higher accuracy at longer distances. Both types cater to sectors such as robotics, gaming, and automotive, enhancing applications like gesture recognition and augmented reality.</p></p>
