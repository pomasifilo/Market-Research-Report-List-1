<p><p>The Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) market encompasses three main types of boilers based on steam parameters. Subcritical Circulating Fluidized Bed boilers operate below critical pressure, offering moderate efficiency and lower costs. Supercritical CFB boilers function above critical pressure, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers push efficiency further by operating at even higher pressures and temperatures, leading to significant fuel savings and lower greenhouse gas emissions. Collectively, these technologies contribute to cleaner energy production in thermal power plants.</p></p>
