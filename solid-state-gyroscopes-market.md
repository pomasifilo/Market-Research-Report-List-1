<p><p>Solid-state gyroscopes are categorized by their axis of measurement: single-axis, dual-axis, and tri-axis. Single-axis gyroscopes measure rotation around one axis and are cost-effective for basic applications. Dual-axis gyroscopes offer rotation detection around two axes, enhancing orientation tracking for more complex systems. Tri-axis gyroscopes detect movement in three dimensions, making them ideal for advanced applications like smartphones, drones, and navigation systems. Each type serves various industries based on precision and complexity requirements.</p></p>
