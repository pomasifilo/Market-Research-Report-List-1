<p><p>The Oil Condition Monitoring System (OCMS) market is categorized into on-site and off-site types. On-site monitoring involves analyzing oil conditions directly at the equipment location, allowing for real-time data collection and immediate decision-making, which enhances operational efficiency and minimizes downtime. Off-site monitoring, on the other hand, entails sampling oil and sending it to specialized labs for analysis. This approach provides detailed assessments and allows for a broader examination of oil properties but may result in longer response times.</p></p>
