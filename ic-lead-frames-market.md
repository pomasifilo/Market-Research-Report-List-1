<p><p>The IC lead frames market is primarily divided into two types based on the manufacturing processes: stamping and etching. Stamping process lead frames involve shaping metal sheets into specific forms using dies, which is efficient for high-volume production. In contrast, etching process lead frames utilize chemical methods to remove material and create intricate designs, offering precision for complex components. Both methods serve the semiconductor industry, catering to different requirements in performance, cost, and application suitability.</p></p>
