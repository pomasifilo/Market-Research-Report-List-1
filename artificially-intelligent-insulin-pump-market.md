<p><p>The Artificially Intelligent Insulin Pump market includes two primary types: normal insulin pumps and patch pumps. Normal insulin pumps are traditional devices that deliver insulin through a tube connected to a catheter inserted under the skin. They offer precise dosage control and real-time monitoring. In contrast, patch pumps are discrete, adhesive devices that attach directly to the skin and provide insulin delivery without tubing. Both types leverage AI to optimize insulin delivery and improve diabetes management by adapting to users' needs.</p></p>
