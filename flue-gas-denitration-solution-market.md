<p><p>The Flue Gas Denitration Solution market encompasses three primary technologies: SNCR (Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction), SCR (Selective Catalytic Reduction), and their combined implementation. SNCR operates at high temperatures, injecting reagents directly into the flue gas for NOx reduction without catalysts. SCR uses catalysts to enhance efficiency at lower temperatures, requiring additional equipment. The combined SNCR/SCR approach optimizes NOx removal across varying conditions, leveraging the strengths of both methods for enhanced performance and flexibility in reducing nitrogen emissions in industrial settings.</p></p>
