<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Intelligent RFID Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF) RFID</li><li>High-Frequency (HF) RFID</li><li>Ultra-high Frequency (UHF) RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent RFID Platform market is segmented into three main types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF) RFID operates at 125-134 kHz, ideal for animal tracking and access control due to its short-range and penetration through materials. High-Frequency (HF) RFID, functioning at 13.56 MHz, is commonly used for items like smart cards and libraries, offering a moderate range and faster data transfer. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) RFID, spanning 300 MHz to 3 GHz, supports long-range applications like supply chain tracking and logistics, enabling rapid inventory management.</p></p>
