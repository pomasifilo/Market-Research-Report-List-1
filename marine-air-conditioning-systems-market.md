<p><strong>The Marine Air Conditioning Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refrigerant Air Conditioners</li><li>Evaporative Air Conditioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine air conditioning systems primarily consist of Refrigerant Air Conditioners and Evaporative Air Conditioners. Refrigerant air conditioners utilize refrigerants to absorb heat from the air, providing efficient cooling essential for maintaining comfort on vessels. Conversely, evaporative air conditioners cool the air by passing it through water-saturated pads, promoting energy efficiency and reduced humidity levels. Both types cater to the unique needs of marine environments, focusing on durability, performance, and energy efficiency to withstand harsh conditions at sea.</p></p>
