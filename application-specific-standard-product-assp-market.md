<p><strong>The Application Specific Standard Product (ASSP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard-cell Designs</li><li>Gate-array and Semi-custom Design</li><li>Full-custom Design</li><li>Structured Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application Specific Standard Products (ASSPs) are integrated circuits designed for specific applications but manufactured using standard processes. Market types include:</p><p>1. **Standard-cell Designs**: Pre-designed logic cells for quick assembly into larger systems.</p><p>2. **Gate-array and Semi-custom Design**: Masks define fixed areas on silicon, allowing users to customize interconnections.</p><p>3. **Full-custom Design**: Tailored designs for high performance, optimized at the transistor level.</p><p>4. **Structured Design**: Combines standard cells with custom elements, balancing flexibility and performance. Each type offers unique benefits for specific application needs.</p></p>
