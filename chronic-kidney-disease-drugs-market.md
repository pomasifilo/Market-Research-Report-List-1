<p><p>The Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) drugs market is categorized into several types, including ACE inhibitors, calcium channel blockers, beta blockers, and others. ACE inhibitors help lower blood pressure and reduce proteinuria, essential for CKD management. Calcium channel blockers aid in controlling hypertension and improving kidney function. Beta blockers are used to manage heart complications related to CKD. Other drugs may include phosphate binders and erythropoiesis-stimulating agents, addressing various aspects of CKD treatment and patient care.</p></p>
