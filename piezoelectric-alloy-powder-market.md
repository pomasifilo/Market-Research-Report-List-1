<p><ul><li>Crystal-Based Piezoelectric Alloy Powder</li><li>Ceramic-Based Piezoelectric Alloy Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric alloy powder market is primarily divided into two types: crystal-based and ceramic-based. Crystal-based piezoelectric alloys, often made from materials like quartz and certain synthesized compounds, exhibit high sensitivity and are used in precision applications such as sensors and actuators. Ceramic-based piezoelectric alloys, typically composed of barium titanate or lead zirconate titanate, are favored for their durability and ease of fabrication, making them suitable for a wide range of electronic devices and transducers.</p></p>
