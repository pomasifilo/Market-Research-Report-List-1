<p><ul><li>Cold Feed Rubber Extruder</li><li>Hot Feed Rubber Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber extruders are specialized machines used to shape rubber materials through a process of forcing them through a die. The market is primarily divided into two types: cold feed and hot feed rubber extruders. Cold feed extruders operate at room temperature, providing better control over material consistency and enabling the processing of various rubber compounds. In contrast, hot feed extruders use heat to soften the rubber, enhancing flow and enabling the processing of bulkier materials, resulting in faster production times.</p></p>
