<p><p>The handheld particle counter market encompasses two main types: airborne and liquid handheld particle counters. Airborne handheld particle counters are designed to measure and analyze the concentration of airborne particles in environments like cleanrooms and manufacturing facilities, ensuring air quality and compliance with industry standards. Conversely, liquid handheld particle counters are utilized for measuring particles in liquids, often applied in pharmaceutical and water quality testing. Both types play crucial roles in contamination control and quality assurance across various industries.</p></p>
