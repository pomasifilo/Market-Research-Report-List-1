<p><p>The gas discharge lamp market is primarily divided into two types: low pressure discharge lamps and high intensity gas discharge (HID) lamps. Low pressure discharge lamps, such as fluorescent lamps, operate at lower pressure and are known for their energy efficiency and long life, making them popular in residential and commercial lighting. High intensity gas discharge lamps, including metal halide and high-pressure sodium lamps, produce intense illumination and are commonly used in outdoor and industrial applications due to their brightness and efficiency.</p></p>
