<p><p>The commercial electricity meters market is primarily divided into single-phase and three-phase electricity meters. Single-phase electricity meters are used for smaller loads, typically residential or small commercial settings, measuring up to 240 volts. In contrast, three-phase electricity meters cater to larger commercial and industrial applications, alternating currents up to 415 volts, providing more efficient energy management for high-capacity systems. Both types play vital roles in accurately monitoring electricity consumption, enabling businesses to optimize energy use and manage costs effectively.</p></p>
