<p><ul><li>Polypropylene Staple Fibre</li><li>Continuous Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene fibre market is primarily divided into two types: polypropylene staple fibre and continuous fibre. Polypropylene staple fibre consists of short strands commonly used in non-woven fabrics, insulation, and apparel, providing durability and lightweight characteristics. In contrast, continuous fibre, produced in long lengths, is utilized in applications requiring strength and resilience, such as ropes, industrial fabrics, and automotive components. Both types are valued for their versatility, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness across various industries.</p></p>
