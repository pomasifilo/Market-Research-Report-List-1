<p><strong>The Industrial Dissolved Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Dissolved Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Electrochemical Dissolved Oxygen Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial dissolved oxygen sensors market comprises two main types: optical dissolved oxygen sensors and electrochemical dissolved oxygen sensors. Optical sensors use luminescence technology to measure oxygen levels and offer advantages like longer lifespan and reduced maintenance. In contrast, electrochemical sensors rely on chemical reactions to detect dissolved oxygen and are known for their sensitivity and cost-effectiveness. Both types are vital for monitoring water quality in various applications, such as wastewater treatment, aquaculture, and chemical processing.</p></p>
