<p><ul><li>Tactile Feedback</li><li>Force Feedback</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Haptic technology is categorized into two primary market types: tactile feedback and force feedback. Tactile feedback involves the use of vibrations or motions to simulate touch sensations, enhancing user experience in applications like gaming, mobile devices, and virtual environments. Conversely, force feedback provides resistance or physical sensations based on user interactions, commonly used in simulation and training systems, such as flight simulators or robotic controls. Together, these technologies create immersive experiences by bridging the gap between digital and physical interactions.</p></p>
