<p><strong>The Front Radar Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Radar</li><li>Millimeter Wave Radar</li><li>Lidar Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The front radar sensor market comprises three main types: Ultrasonic Radar, Millimeter Wave Radar, and Lidar Radar. Ultrasonic Radar operates using sound waves to detect proximity and obstacles, making it suitable for low-speed applications. Millimeter Wave Radar utilizes electromagnetic waves for long-range detection, commonly used in automotive applications for adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance. Lidar Radar employs laser pulses to create high-resolution 3D maps, enhancing object recognition and environment perception, particularly in autonomous driving scenarios.</p></p>
