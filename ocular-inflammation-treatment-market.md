<p><p>The ocular inflammation treatment market comprises various therapeutic types targeting eye inflammation. **Immunosuppressants** reduce immune responses, while **monoclonal antibodies** specifically target inflammatory pathways. **Corticosteroids** are potent anti-inflammatories that alleviate swelling and pain. **Cycloplegic agents** temporarily paralyze ciliary muscles to relieve pain. **Antibiotics** treat secondary infections, and **analgesics** manage pain. **Antivirals** and **antifungals** address specific viral and fungal infections contributing to ocular inflammation, ensuring comprehensive treatment options for different underlying causes.</p></p>
