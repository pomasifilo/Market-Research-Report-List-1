<p><p>The neurostimulation devices market encompasses various technologies aimed at modulating neural activity. Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) targets specific brain regions for disorders like Parkinson's. Vagus Nerve Stimulation (VNS) influences the vagus nerve, often for epilepsy and depression treatment. Responsive Neurostimulation (RNS) adapts stimulation based on detected brain activity. Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) utilizes magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells, primarily for depression. Spinal Cord Stimulation (SCS) alleviates chronic pain by stimulating the spinal cord, while Peripheral Nerve Stimulation (PNS) targets peripheral nerves for pain relief.</p></p>
