<p><p>The automotive wind deflectors market comprises primarily two types: metal and plastic. Metal wind deflectors, often made from aluminum or stainless steel, offer durability and a premium aesthetic, providing effective wind resistance but may add weight to the vehicle. Conversely, plastic wind deflectors are lightweight, cost-effective, and flexible in design, often made from acrylic or polycarbonate, making them popular for aftermarket applications. Both types serve to enhance aerodynamics, reduce noise, and improve driving comfort by deflecting airflow away from windows.</p></p>
