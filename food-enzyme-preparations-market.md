Lipase facilitates fat digestion, crucial for flavor development in dairy and bakery items. Redox enzymes play a vital role in oxidative processes, impacting color and freshness. Other enzyme preparations include cellulose and lactase, catering to diverse food applications, including brewing and dairy processing.</p></p>
