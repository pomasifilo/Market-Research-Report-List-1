<p><p>The EV battery test system market is categorized into three main types: Unit Test, Module Test, and Pack Test. Unit testing focuses on evaluating individual battery cells for performance and safety. Module testing assesses groups of cells within a module, ensuring reliability and efficiency. Pack testing examines the entire battery pack, validating its overall functionality and integration within the electric vehicle. Each testing type is crucial for quality assurance, enhancing battery performance, and ensuring safety in electric vehicles.</p></p>
