<p><strong>The Wavefront Aberrometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavefront guided LASIK</li><li>Wavefront-optimized LASIK</li><li>Topography LASIK</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavefront aberrrometry encompasses three main types in the LASIK market. Wavefront-guided LASIK utilizes detailed wavefront data to customize the laser treatment for individual visual aberrations, offering precision in correcting refractive errors. Wavefront-optimized LASIK focuses on pre-existing corneal structures to minimize post-operative visual disturbances, emphasizing overall corneal health. Topography LASIK employs corneal topography maps to guide the laser, ensuring accurate reshaping tailored to the unique contour of the cornea, enhancing visual outcomes while addressing irregularities.</p></p>
