<p><p>The Moisture Barrier Fabric market is primarily divided into two types: breathable and non-breathable fabrics. Breathable moisture barrier fabrics allow vapor to escape while preventing water penetration, making them ideal for applications requiring comfort and moisture management, such as activewear and outdoor gear. Conversely, non-breathable moisture barrier fabrics provide a stronger barrier against liquid infiltration but trap humidity, making them suitable for protective clothing and industrial applications where water resistance is critical. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
