Lower extremity pumps target the legs and feet, facilitating venous return and preventing clot formation, especially post-surgery or during prolonged immobility. Upper extremity pumps focus on the arms, enhancing circulation for patients at risk due to surgery or conditions affecting the upper body. Both types are essential for patient safety and recovery.</p></p>
