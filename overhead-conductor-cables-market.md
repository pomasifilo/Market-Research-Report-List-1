<p><p>The Overhead Conductor Cables market encompasses several types of conductors for power transmission. AAC (All Aluminium Conductor) is lightweight and highly conductive, ideal for short spans. AAAC (All Aluminium Alloy Conductor) combines good conductivity with higher strength, enhancing durability against environmental factors. ACSR (Aluminium Conductor Steel Reinforced) features steel reinforcement for added tensile strength, making it suitable for long-distance transmission. Other types may include specialized conductors designed for specific applications, offering additional benefits in various electrical infrastructure projects.</p></p>
