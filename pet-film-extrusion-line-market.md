<p><p>The PET film extrusion line market is primarily categorized into multilayer and single-layer types. In contrast, multilayer PET films incorporate multiple layers, enhancing barriers, strength, and functionality, making them ideal for specialized applications such as food packaging and medical supplies. This distinction allows manufacturers to choose the appropriate technology based on performance requirements and production capabilities.</p></p>
