<p><strong>The Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid applied waterproofing membranes market includes three primary types: bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious. Bituminous membranes are made from asphalt, offering excellent durability and adhesion, commonly used in roofing and below-grade applications. Elastomeric membranes provide flexibility and are resistant to UV rays and temperature fluctuations, making them ideal for various structures. Cementitious membranes, composed of cement-based materials, are easy to apply and cure, suitable for basements and water tanks due to their strong adhesion and waterproof properties.</p></p>
