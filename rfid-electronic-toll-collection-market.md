<p><ul><li>Perimeter-Based Charges</li><li>Time-Based Charges</li><li>Point-Based Charges</li><li>Distance-Based Charges</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID electronic toll collection market comprises several charge types. Perimeter-based charges involve tolls for entering or exiting a designated area. Time-based charges impose fees based on the duration spent in a toll zone. Point-based charges apply at specific toll points along a route, while distance-based charges calculate fees based on the distance traveled within a toll network. Other methods may include flat fees or discounts for frequent users. Each type aims to enhance efficiency in toll collection and traffic management.</p></p>
