<p><p>Cell-based humanized mouse models are vital for preclinical research, enabling the study of human biology and diseases. CD34 humanized models are created by engrafting human hematopoietic stem cells, useful for studying blood disorders and immune responses. PBMC humanized models involve transplanting peripheral blood mononuclear cells, ideal for immunology and infectious disease research. BLT humanized models combine bone marrow, liver, and thymus transplantation, facilitating comprehensive studies on human immune system development and function, bridging gaps in understanding human-specific disease mechanisms.</p></p>
