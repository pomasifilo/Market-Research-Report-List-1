<p><ul><li>Manual Pressure Infusor</li><li>Automatic Pressure Infusor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure infusor market comprises two primary types: manual and automatic pressure infusors. Manual pressure infusors require healthcare providers to exert physical force to maintain pressure, allowing for controlled infusion during medical procedures. In contrast, automatic pressure infusors utilize automated mechanisms to regulate pressure, offering consistent and precise infusion rates. Both types serve critical roles in delivering fluids and medications efficiently in clinical settings, with choices depending on factors such as ease of use, accuracy, and patient requirements.</p></p>
