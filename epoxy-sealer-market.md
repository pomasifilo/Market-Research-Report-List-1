<p><strong>The Epoxy Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy sealer market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based epoxy sealers are known for their low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for indoor applications. They offer easy application and quick drying times. In contrast, solvent-based epoxy sealers provide superior durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for industrial use. However, they emit higher VOC levels and require careful handling. Each type serves distinct customer needs based on application and environmental considerations.</p></p>
